About me

I started my adventure with the construction industry in high school.

During the holiday season, I earned my first money working as a helper in renovating houses and flats. During my studies, I established cooperation with a large construction enterprise, implementing projects throughout Poland. During the interruption between the semesters, I worked for this company on medium and large facilities, carrying out work in the field of carpentry, assembly of partition walls and ceilings constructions, mounting plasterboards, puttying, plastering, painting and many other finishing works. It was a real school of life for me.

The company’s history

Offer

We have comprehensive experience in the field of finishing works for new buildings, as well as renovations.

The company specializes in the installation of system ceilings from reputable companies - Rockfon, Armstrong, OWA, Ecophon. Forthermore, we deal with the installation of Hera Design acoustic ceiling tiles and the installation of Derako cladding products.

The company also deals with the installation of partition walls from plasterboard on aluminium and wooden constructions, as well as building carpentry.

We are characterized by reliability, professionalism and creativity. We always work safe and with care for the environment. We have a VCA safety certificate at the VOL level.

The company also provides services of attestation and certification of power equipment in accordance with the European standard NEN 3140.
